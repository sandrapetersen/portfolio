Rabarberbladet

A website that reflects a local genre


Rabarberbladet is a local newspaper writing about breaking news and other stuff from the Nørrebro area in Copenhagen. Rabarberbladet wanted a website so more people could read the newspaper without holding the physical one.


In close collaboration with four fellow students, we designed a web site for Rabarberbladet. It was essential to keep the newspaper visual look and feeling, moreover the old and humorous genre that the editorial team has given the news paper.


During the design process we used several tools to would keep the prosepctive user in our mind and what would make the site usable and desireable. We held several meetings we the editorial team through the process, in order to keep them up to date with what we would designed and to be provided with feedback for our thoughts.


The final website we holds a breaking news site, where we used a card layout to show the latests articles. Moreover, did we made an archive site for older articles, an about and contact site.
