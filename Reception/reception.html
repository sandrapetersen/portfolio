Reception

Graphic layout for the magazine Reception


Reception is a magazine writing about Nordic litterature, where the texts and peoms are written by upcoming authors. The editorial team consists of voluenteer students from the University of Copenhagen. For two years I was the layouter for the magazine. Togheter with a coworker, I was responsible for the visual expression, setup, and to prepare the magazine for printing.
